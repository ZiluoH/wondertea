About Wonder Tea

Wonder Tea is boba tea shop in San Francisco's sunset district specializing in Cheese Creama & Fruit Tea. We dedicate ourselves to serving high quality bubble milk tea. This will be achieved by using fresh and high quality ingredients and strictly following preparation guidelines. Wonder Tea also offer delicious snacks such as popcorn chicken and eggettes.
